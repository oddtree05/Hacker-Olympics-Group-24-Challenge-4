About

Welcome to Bone Apple Tea, a food review blog located in the GTA. Four foodies came together to review the best (and worst) of restaurants and provide our honest reviews.



Our ratings are based on numerous factors. Although personal taste comes to play, we also factored in price, food quality, customer service, interior design and accessibility to the restaurants. These are placed on a scale from 1 (terrible) to 5 (Michelin star worthy).

Fuwa Fuwa

More jiggly than Jigglypuff.

Fuwa Fuwa has many locations in Toronto, but we went to the Eglinton location. We were greeted by friendly servers and a large array of toppings for Japanese pancakes. Never having tried these pancakes before, we were overwhelmed by the options but the servers gladly helped us. Seeing these all over Instagram, we had high expectations. However, the pancakes were just mediocre and slightly expensive. Rainey actually makes better pancakes so we will be staying at home for our Japanese pancake needs instead. Overall, a good one time experience, but we wouldn't visit again.

  • Date: January 2020
  • Pros: Very trendy. Very jiggly. Very thick. Great for your next IG post.
  • Cons: Over hyped. Rainey believes that she can do it better.

The French

It is not just for french people!

The French was a fantastic experience. From the moment we all walked in, we felt the pink colors on the wall and chairs jump out, adding to the overall aesthetic of the restaurant. We were greeted by friendly servers who took their time to get to know us. The quality of the French food was amazing, and the plating was perfect for posting on Instagram! The overall ambience of the restaurant was great, making it a quiet place for groups to have conversations with one another. Overall, great experience and we will be coming back when we are in the Hamilton area!

  • Date: January 2021
  • Pros: Very aesthetic. Plating of the dishes are very elegant.
  • Cons: Slightly expensive.
